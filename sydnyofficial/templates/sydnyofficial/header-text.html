With his dark sultry vocals, captivating storytelling, and catchy rhythms, Sydny Dre is a fast-rising singer/songwriter, multi-instrumentalist and music producer from Nigeria telling his story and sharing his perspective with the world through relatable music. Armed with poetic lyricism and a sexy, chill sound that complements his personality, his unique blend of afro-fusion born from his afro-percussive roots as a child-drummer and teenage enthusiasm for Hip-hop and R&B, is unlike any other that exists or has existed before. He is definitely an artist to watch out for as he leaves memorable impressions on his listeners in every song.
